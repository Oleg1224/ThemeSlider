PlayStation 5 (PS5) is the latest gaming console from Sony that delivers a high-quality gaming experience with advanced graphics, an improved controller and support for the latest technology.

Xbox Series X and Xbox Series S are two new generation gaming consoles from Microsoft. The Series X is a more powerful console, delivering high resolution and frame rates, while the Series S is a more affordable version with minor compromises in resolution and storage. Both consoles offer high performance and are backwards compatible with games from previous generations of Xbox.

Nintendo Switch is a hybrid gaming console published by Nintendo. It allows you to play both at home by connecting the console to the TV, and on the go, in portable console mode. The Switch features a unique design of detachable controllers called Joy-Cons and supports a wide range of games, including Nintendo exclusive titles, indie games and third-party titles.

Razer is a company that produces high quality gaming equipment. PC (personal computer) is a computer for individual use that can be configured at your discretion.
